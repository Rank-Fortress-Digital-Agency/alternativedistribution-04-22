What number is Taurus in the zodiac?

Taurus is the second sign of the zodiac. People born under the sign are typically known for their patience, strength, and steadiness. They are often good at taking things slow and making sure everything is done correctly. As a result, Taurus people can be very reliable in both personal and professional contexts.

What is the Taurus Zodiac Sign?

The Taurus zodiac sign is located in the first house of the horoscope and is ruled by Venus. People born under this sign are typically sensual, earthy, and practical. They are also confident and enjoy a good sense of humour. They are loyal to those around them and enjoy spending time with family and friends. The Taurus personality typically favours stability over change, so they may be slow to open up emotionally or financially. As a result, it is important for them to surround themselves with people who support their values and who will offer stability in their lives.
